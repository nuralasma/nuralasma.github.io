Privacy Policy

This policy explains what information the Nur Al-Asma Al-Husna app handles and how it is used.

Effective date: June 15, 2026

Overview

At Nur Al-Asma Al-Husna, we value your privacy. This application is designed to provide a contemplative experience without intrusive data collection. The app does not require an account and does not contain advertising or sell personal information.

Local Data Storage

Your personal interactions with the app, including settings, saved items, and viewed-reflection history, are stored strictly on your device. We do not sync this data to a cloud account. You can remove locally stored data by clearing the app's storage or uninstalling the app.

User Input

You normally interact with the app by selecting from curated Attributes and Topics. No personal text or identifying information is required for these interactions, and your selections are not shared as a personal profile.

Diagnostics

We use Firebase Crashlytics to improve app stability. Crashlytics may process diagnostic information such as crash logs, app version, device type, operating-system details, and the app's state when an error occurs. This data is not intended to identify you and is used only to understand and fix technical problems.

Analytics

We use Firebase Analytics to understand how the app is used, including which Names of Allah are most frequently explored. Analytics may also process general technical and usage information, such as app opens, app version, device type, operating-system information, approximate region, and an app instance identifier. We review this information in aggregate to improve the app and do not use it to identify you by name.

Content Reporting

The app allows you to report content for review. If you choose to include an optional comment in a report, that comment and the information needed to identify the reported content are sent to our review team. Please do not include your name, contact details, or other sensitive personal information in a report comment.

Report information is used only to review the content, address quality or accuracy concerns, and protect the integrity of the app.

Service Providers and Sharing

We do not sell or rent personal information. Google may process technical, analytics, and diagnostic information as the provider of Firebase Analytics, Firebase Crashlytics, and Google Play services. Optional report comments are shared only with services and team members needed to receive and review the report. Information may also be disclosed when required by law.

Data Retention and Your Choices

Local app data remains on your device until you clear the app's storage or uninstall the app. Analytics and diagnostic information is retained according to the settings of the relevant Firebase service. Optional report comments are kept only as long as reasonably needed to review and address the report.

Because the app does not create user accounts or maintain cloud profiles, there is no app account to delete. You may contact us with questions or requests concerning a report comment you submitted.

Children's Privacy

The app does not knowingly request directly identifying personal information from children. If you believe a child included personal information in a content report or support message, please contact us so we can review and delete it when appropriate.

Security

We use reasonable measures intended to protect information handled by the app. However, no method of electronic storage or internet transmission can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated when the app's features or data practices change. The updated policy will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.

Contact

For privacy questions or support, email support@nuralasmaalhusna.com.
