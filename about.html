About the app

A quiet space for remembering Allah through His Beautiful Names and carrying that awareness into daily life.

Our Purpose

Allah tells us:

“Surely, it is in the remembrance of Allah that hearts can find comfort.” (Qur'an 13:28)

Nur Al-Asma Al-Husna seeks to help believers remember Allah through His Beautiful Names and Attributes. By reflecting on His Attributes and acting upon the guidance they inspire, we hope to strengthen our connection with Allah, cultivate a deeper love for Him, and find greater peace, purpose, and spiritual growth in our daily lives.

In a world filled with distractions and competing priorities, our aim is to encourage hearts to turn toward Allah and to give greater importance to faith, righteousness, and the life to come.

How the App Helps

Each reflection connects one of Allah's Attributes to a real-life situation and offers practical guidance that can be put into action. The goal is not only to learn about Allah's Attributes, but to allow them to shape our thoughts, refine our character, and inspire positive change in our actions.

Our Approach

The reflections are intended to:

  • Encourage remembrance of Allah throughout the day.
  • Inspire practical acts of worship, gratitude, compassion, and service.
  • Develop qualities that reflect the guidance found in Allah's Attributes.
  • Help connect everyday experiences to spiritual growth.
  • Encourage believers to prioritize their relationship with Allah over temporary worldly concerns.

A Gentle Reminder

These reflections are intended for personal contemplation and inspiration. They are not religious rulings (fatwas) and should not replace the study of the Qur'an, Hadith, or guidance from qualified scholars.

May Allah Guide Us

May Allah help us recognize His Attributes, remember Him often, draw closer to Him through sincere action, and make faith a guiding light in every aspect of our lives. Ameen.
